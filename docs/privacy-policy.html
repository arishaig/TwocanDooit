Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TwocanDooit! We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains how TwocanDooit ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Data Only

TwocanDooit is designed as a privacy-first application. All your personal data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2.2 Data We Store Locally

  • Routine Data: Your custom routines, steps, and configurations
  • Usage Analytics: Run counts, completion rates, and timing data for your routines
  • App Settings: Your preferences for audio, notifications, and app behavior
  • Progress Data: Your routine execution history and completion records

2.3 Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Usage data to external servers
  • Analytics or crash reports to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

Your locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Provide routine management and execution features
  • Track your progress and completion statistics
  • Maintain your app settings and preferences
  • Enable routine import/export functionality

4. Data Sharing and Export

4.1 User-Initiated Sharing

You can voluntarily export and share your routines using the app's export feature. When you export routines:

  • Only routine structure and steps are included
  • Personal analytics and run data are excluded
  • You control when and with whom you share exported data

4.2 No Automatic Data Sharing

We do not automatically share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

5. Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms
  • We use industry-standard encryption for sensitive data
  • No data is transmitted over the internet without your explicit action
  • App permissions are minimized to only what's necessary for functionality

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Firebase

We use Firebase for basic app infrastructure, but not for data collection:

  • Firebase is configured for minimal data collection
  • No personal data is sent to Firebase
  • No analytics or crash reporting is enabled

6.2 Audio Assets

The app includes audio files sourced from Freesound.org under Creative Commons licenses. These are static assets and do not involve any data collection.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can clear your routine data at any time through the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data
  • We do not retain any data after app uninstallation

8. Children's Privacy

TwocanDooit is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13, and our local-only data storage approach ensures no personal data leaves the device.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any data at any time
  • Portability: You can export your routines for backup or sharing
  • Deletion: You can clear all data or uninstall the app

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Provide notice of significant changes through the app or on this website
  • Maintain the same privacy-first principles

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or TwocanDooit's privacy practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub: Create an issue
  • Email: [Your contact email]

12. Commitment to Privacy

TwocanDooit was created with neurodivergent individuals in mind, and we understand the importance of privacy and trust. Our commitment to keeping your data local and secure is fundamental to our mission of providing a safe, supportive tool for routine management.
